Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mentora and how does it work?

Mentora is a student-led volunteer tutoring initiative from Bishkek International School. We connect motivated students with volunteer tutors who provide free, high-quality academic support. Our platform makes it easy to find the right tutor for your needs and schedule sessions that work for you.

Who can become a volunteer tutor?

Any motivated student from Bishkek International School can apply to become a volunteer tutor. We look for students who are passionate about helping others, have strong academic performance, and can commit to regular tutoring sessions. No formal teaching experience is required - we provide training and support to all our volunteers.

How do I sign up for tutoring sessions?

Getting started is easy! Browse our available tutors, read their profiles and reviews, and contact them directly through our platform. You can schedule a free trial session to see if it's a good fit. Most tutors offer flexible scheduling and can accommodate different time zones and learning preferences.

What subjects and levels do you cover?

We offer tutoring in a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, English, Science, History, and more. Our tutors cover various academic levels from elementary school through high school. Each tutor's profile shows their specific subjects and experience levels, so you can find the perfect match for your learning needs.

Become a Volunteer Teacher

Share your knowledge, inspire others, and make a real difference. Join Mentora as a volunteer teacher and help students across Kyrgyzstan reach their potential!
